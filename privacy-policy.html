Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to our Food Delivery App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our services, including authentication via Facebook Login.

Information We Collect

  • Facebook Login Data: When you sign in with Facebook, we collect your email, name, and profile picture.
  • App-Specific Data: We collect data related to your orders, cart items, and preferences to provide personalized services.
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with our app, such as pages visited and features used.

How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide access to your account.
  • Order Processing: To process your food orders and manage your cart.
  • Personalization: To offer discounts based on your loyalty points and improve your experience.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to process payments (e.g., via Stripe) or fulfill deliveries. We may share anonymized data for analytics purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored in our MongoDB database and protected with industry-standard security measures.

Data Deletion Instructions

We comply with Facebook’s Platform Policy, GDPR, and CCPA by allowing you to request the deletion of your personal data. To permanently delete your account and all associated information, please follow these steps:

  1. Email Us: Send an email to support@your-app.com from the email address associated with your Food Delivery App account (the same email used for Facebook Login).
  2. Subject Line: Use “Data Deletion Request” as the subject line to ensure quick processing.
  3. Include Details: In the email, include your full name and confirm you want to delete your account. We may ask you to verify your identity for security (e.g., by confirming account details).
  4. Processing Time: We will process your request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email once your data is deleted.

Upon deletion, we will remove the following data from our MongoDB database:

  • Facebook Login Data: Your email, name, and profile picture collected during authentication.
  • App-Specific Data: Cart items, order history, loyalty points, and user preferences.
  • Other Information: Any additional data you provided, such as delivery addresses (if applicable).

Note: Anonymized data used for analytics, which cannot be linked to you, may be retained as permitted by law.

Your Privacy Rights

Under GDPR (for EU users) and CCPA (for California users), you have rights to access, update, or delete your personal data.
